# Sourcing — mounting materials

Neon flex is sorted; this covers the **panel + fixings**.

## Panel — gloss black exterior compact phenolic (HPL)
Ask for: **gloss black, exterior grade, ~13–18 mm, with fire certificate** (for the dovetail
groove you need ~13–18 mm — most online listings are 6–8 mm cladding).

- **Trespa Meteon** — https://www.trespa.com/en_US/Trespa-meteon
  - Gloss black = **Lumen** (high-gloss) finish; matt/satin black = Uni Colours **A90.0.0**
  - Colour card (PDF): https://www.trespa.com/dam/jcr:99d27146-b397-41bf-814b-ac21e80442e8/144154_COLOURCARD_TrespaMeteon_Online_HR_no_spot%20(3).pdf
  - Samples direct: **+31 495 721 424**
- **Fundermax Max Compact Exterior** — https://www.fundermax.com/en/Portfolio/Exterior/Max%20Compact%20Exterior
- **Buy in Sweden (stocks Trespa + Fundermax, cuts to size):** **Vink** — https://vink.se/
- Cut-to-size online (black Trespa, EU — 6 mm): https://plasticsheetsshop.co.uk/product/trespa-meteon-panel-black-6mm-ral-9005/

~Cost: 37–57k kr for ~45 m². Routable on the Shaper (abrasive → carbide bits + dust).

## Mounting hardware (brackets / fixings) — stainless or alu

- **Würth** — https://www.wuerth.se (stainless fasteners)
